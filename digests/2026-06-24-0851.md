## 🏆 Top Stories

1.  **Five9 Launches New Voice AI Agents and AI Agent Studio for Agentic Self-Service.** (2026-06-23)
    Five9 introduced an updated release of its Voice AI Agents and a new AI Agent Studio at Customer Contact Week in Las Vegas. This initiative aims to move enterprises beyond traditional IVR and scripted bots by enabling agentic AI for complex customer self-service interactions, with capabilities for multi-agent orchestration, human-like voice, and context-rich handoffs. The new Studio provides a unified environment for building, testing, deploying, and monitoring these agents, including features like integrated call testing, versioning, and automated post-call AI evaluations.
    **Why it matters:** This is a significant product push from a major CCaaS incumbent into the agentic AI space, directly targeting self-service automation. While it addresses a critical market need, it also highlights Five9's ongoing pivot from a seat-based model, which is structurally exposed to AI deflection. The newness of their "agentic self-service" and governance features (AI Agent Studio) presents an opportunity to question their battle-tested reliability and cost predictability compared to established pure-play platforms.

2.  **Glean Recognized as Gartner "Market Shaper" and Integrates with TinyFish for Live Web Data.** (2026-06-22, 2026-06-24)
    Glean was named a "Market Shaper" in the 2026 Gartner Emerging Market Quadrant for No-Code Agent Builders - Startup Vendors, recognized for its approach to exposing enterprise context, tools, and governance as a shared enterprise agent layer. Additionally, Glean announced an integration with TinyFish, allowing its enterprise AI agents to access live data from the open web, including searching public sources, fetching web pages, extracting structured data, and executing web workflows, all while operating within Glean's secure knowledge environment.
    **Why it matters:** Glean continues to gain traction and analyst recognition, albeit in the employee-facing/internal knowledge management space. The TinyFish integration enhances its ability to ground internal AI agents with real-time external information. For Kore.ai, this reinforces the need to clearly differentiate our leadership in *customer-facing* conversational AI and the unique requirements of external CX, where reliability, determinism, and seamless omnichannel are paramount.
