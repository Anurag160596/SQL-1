# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Wednesday, 24 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are emphasizing AI agent capabilities and integrations, but significant gaps remain in enterprise-grade governance, true omnichannel voice, and predictable TCO, areas where Kore.ai's ABL and platform depth offer a clear advantage.

## Three signals that matter

### Five9 pivots to "Agentic CX" with new Voice AI Agents
**What happened —** Five9 launched a new release of its Voice AI Agents, positioning them as "Agentic CX" and aiming to replace traditional IVR systems. [Five9 Launches Breakthrough New Release of Voice AI Agents - Directors Club News -](https://www.directorsclubnews.com/news/five9-launches-breakthrough-new-release-of-voice-ai-agents/) (2026-06-24)
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This highlights the market's continued push towards agentic capabilities. However, Five9's seat-based model and historical focus on IVR replacement, rather than true omnichannel agentic orchestration, present an opening. Kore.ai's ABL provides deterministic, omnichannel agentic capabilities with clear cost-per-outcome, contrasting with Five9's potential for unpredictable overages and a less integrated experience.

### Glean and TinyFish partner to enable AI agents on live web data
**What happened —** Glean is partnering with TinyFish to allow AI agents to act on live web data within secure enterprise contexts, bridging the gap to the live web for enterprise AI agents. [TinyFish and Glean partner to enable AI agents to act on live web data within secure enterprise contexts. - Pluang -](https://pluang.com/news/tinyfish-and-glean-partner-to-enable-ai-agents-to-act-on-live-web-data-within-secure-enterprise-contexts) (2026-06-24)
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This move by Glean underscores the growing demand for AI agents to interact with dynamic data. However, Glean's core strength remains in enterprise search and internal knowledge management, not customer-facing CX. Kore.ai's platform offers robust, secure, and auditable integration capabilities for customer-facing AI agents, with a proven track record in regulated industries, differentiating from Glean's employee-facing focus.

### Salesforce Agentforce pushes agentic AI to "Retail Revenue Reality"
**What happened —** Salesforce's Agentforce is being positioned to move agentic AI from hype to revenue reality in retail, with a focus on transforming marketing workflows and driving business agility. [Salesforce’s Agentforce Commerce Pushes Agentic AI From Hype to Retail Revenue Reality - The Futurum Group -](https://futurumgroup.com/salesforces-agentforce-commerce-pushes-agentic-ai-from-hype-to-retail-revenue-reality/) (2026-06-24)
**What it means for Kore.ai —** Salesforce is aggressively marketing Agentforce for revenue generation, but their pricing complexity and reliance on the Salesforce ecosystem create potential lock-in and TCO concerns. Kore.ai's provider-agnostic approach and transparent, deterministic pricing model offer a compelling alternative for enterprises seeking flexibility and predictable ROI, especially when integrating with diverse backend systems.

## Watch for
- Continued evolution of agentic AI capabilities from major cloud providers and CCaaS vendors.
- Any further analyst evaluations that might shift rankings in the conversational AI for CX space.
- Developments in voice AI and natural language understanding that could impact agent performance and customer experience.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's leadership in enterprise-grade governance and auditability, particularly for regulated industries, contrasting with the inherent probabilistic nature of LLM-native agents.
- Highlight the cost predictability and ROI of Kore.ai's ABL and per-outcome pricing model against the consumption-based or seat-based models of competitors, which can lead to unpredictable TCO.
- Reinforce Kore.ai's true omnichannel capabilities, especially in voice, as a key differentiator against voice-first or voice-limited solutions.
- Leverage analyst validation (Gartner, Forrester) to position Kore.ai as a proven Leader in Conversational AI for Customer Service, distinguishing from competitors with weaker or no CX-specific analyst recognition.
- Arm sales teams with specific questions to probe competitors on their deterministic execution, cross-agent tracing, and true omnichannel session continuity.
