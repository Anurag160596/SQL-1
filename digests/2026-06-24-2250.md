# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Wednesday, 24 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Five9's launch of Voice AI Agents and Salesforce's Agentforce Commerce push underscore the growing importance of agentic AI in customer experience, but both vendors' approaches are vulnerable to Kore.ai's strengths in determinism, cost-per-outcome, and omnichannel capabilities.

## Three signals that matter
### Five9 Enters Voice AI Market
**What happened —** (2026-06-24) Five9 launched its Voice AI Agents to replace traditional contact center IVR systems, as reported by CX Today and Directors Club News.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This move highlights Five9's attempt to expand its offerings, but its seat-based pricing model and limited voice capabilities create an opening for Kore.ai to emphasize its cost-per-outcome approach and true omnichannel support.

### Salesforce Expands Agentforce
**What happened —** (2026-06-24) Salesforce announced Agentforce Commerce, pushing agentic AI from hype to retail revenue reality, as reported by The Futurum Group and Techzine Global.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** Salesforce's expansion of Agentforce highlights the growing importance of agentic AI in customer experience, but its pricing complexity and consumption-based model create an opportunity for Kore.ai to emphasize its predictable cost-per-outcome approach.

### ServiceNow Enhances Agent Platform
**What happened —** (2026-06-24) Inspira became a full-spectrum ServiceNow partner, rolling out its own AI Control Tower, as reported by iTWire.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** ServiceNow's enhanced Agent Platform and partnership with Inspira demonstrate its commitment to agentic AI, but its ITSM-centric approach and limited voice capabilities create an opening for Kore.ai to emphasize its customer experience focus and omnichannel support.

## Watch for
* Further developments in Five9's Voice AI Agents and their potential impact on the market
* Salesforce's continued expansion of Agentforce and its potential to disrupt traditional contact center models
* ServiceNow's progress in enhancing its Agent Platform and potential partnerships in the customer experience space

## Recommended actions
* Emphasize Kore.ai's determinism and cost-per-outcome approach in marketing and sales efforts
* Develop targeted messaging to highlight the limitations of Five9's seat-based pricing model and Salesforce's consumption-based model
* Explore potential partnerships with companies in the customer experience space to further enhance Kore.ai's offerings
