# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Saturday, 27 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are pushing AI agent capabilities, with Salesforce launching a pay-per-resolution model and ServiceNow integrating AI agents more deeply. Kore.ai's ABL and compile-time validation offer a clear counter to the inherent risks of prompt-based development and unpredictable consumption pricing.

## Three signals that matter

## Watch for
- Continued evolution of Salesforce's Agentforce pricing models and enterprise adoption of pay-per-resolution.
- Deeper integration announcements between ServiceNow and Google Cloud regarding their AI agent capabilities.
- Cresta's ability to translate sports partnerships into broader enterprise CX wins.

## Recommended actions
- Arm Sales with clear talking points on the unpredictability and ambiguity of "pay-per-resolution" models versus Kore.ai's deterministic, per-interaction cost attribution.
- Highlight Kore.ai's model-agnostic ABL as a key differentiator against platform-specific AI agent integrations like ServiceNow/Google Cloud, emphasizing flexibility and avoiding vendor lock-in.
- Continue to emphasize Kore.ai's leadership in analyst evaluations for Conversational AI Platforms, particularly in regulated industries, as a counter to niche or vertical-specific solutions.
