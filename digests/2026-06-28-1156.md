# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Sunday, 28 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Salesforce's launch of a new pay-per-resolution agent introduces both competitive pricing pressure and potential TCO unpredictability, while ServiceNow's deeper integration with Google Cloud signals growing ambition in autonomous enterprise operations. These moves underscore the critical need for Kore.ai to emphasize its predictable, governed, and analyst-validated agentic platform to build enterprise trust.

## Three signals that matter

### Agentforce launches new pay-per-resolution model
**What happened —** (2026-06-25) Salesforce launched its "Agentforce Help Agent" for service, featuring a pay-per-resolution pricing model and rapid deployment capabilities, with analysis noting its setup strength but vagueness on financial specifics and trust implications for enterprises [Salesforce](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMigAFBVV95cUxPZVRVaWpYRzZzWGVzZlJXOS1TYWcyUTU4QkZFNHQ2ZTRVd2hpcGd2VGhIUVZvdXJTTTVxS1JvOFNsTDI4eC1md1hUUklVMjRFcGRBbkUyN0tMcGJjMEZDYTdQdkpESGlfc18ySjV1TGhUVXV4NmFVcmRxa1ZyZnF0Nw), [The Futurum Group](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMixwFBVV95cUxOX09ieFc0MjAwTGdNbFNYVEwzcHEzZVZTR3lldEIxQ2o4UEc2TDhTWGRfd0FwZi0waVdVU2E1d1IySDNIMkVLUUxtRXRUWWNMek1Lc1BvQ25oQXpZcEZJZkFsUEREVHV3WU5VaEk5UHBPZ2VOZGRhQzlsS1BTMktPRUpxRXVULS1Eb19RcTJ5UWp6bF9tX1RlMkl1UE5BZVhyVGJWTjJxaDhURjlKNmhmcURiMVJNVUlxREJaSXc0S085Z3VvN3Vj).
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This new pricing model from a major player like Salesforce creates both competitive pressure and an opening. Kore.ai should highlight ABL's precise per-agent/intent/tool cost attribution and deterministic execution, contrasting with the potential unpredictability and "what counts as a resolution?" ambiguity of pay-per-resolution models.

### ServiceNow deepens autonomous agent ecosystem with Google Cloud
**What happened —** (2026-06-26) ServiceNow announced a partnership with Google Cloud to unite AI agents for autonomous enterprise operations, while a separate report raised questions about the governance of AI agents when workflows cross platforms HPCwire, [ERP Today](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMid0FVX3lxTE9kWXZfRVVubVk1MU1KcnJ3UzB1cUh5ZEsyc1A5RXo5dTRnWXR0T3ZUdGxWRkROMnQxVDNDM3J3cFZaRnhWdG1uZlh5SThyUGE3Y0RRbGJXYjlHUTRwdXdKM0dpTXl2ZmJNRnpqMGRtR3M5UUFtLW5n).
**What it means for Kore.ai —** ServiceNow's strategic partnership signals aggressive expansion in autonomous agents, but their ITSM-centric heritage and "assists" consumption model still present TCO and CX-specific integration challenges. Kore.ai can counter by emphasizing its full-scope, omnichannel platform with compile-time validation and deterministic execution for complex, governed CX workflows.

### Parloa survey highlights widespread chatbot avoidance
**What happened —** (2026-06-25) A survey sponsored by Parloa revealed that over half of Americans actively avoid chatbots, indicating persistent user skepticism towards current conversational AI solutions [NewsBytes](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMiswFBVV95cUxPbTA4UnFoZUY3Tjc3VkhUVHY4QUFVSUVNWnBIUnV0YnNtX012T1RRLUNrWkVXU2x6MjgxdXhta1ZsM21KMnVfWHpWcFF5RVZvNlNoVmtzU05iVlBITzhrVk9tNTRRX1RtZ0VOU3JyX3VITlo1a20tNUJxdEdnWi1JVFJZelJGOVo3bUU1clBCaXJaR0JKSTBDOTJkT1NkQWd4UlhRNnNRWFF5Mkl1ZWdnOUNaQQ).
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This market sentiment reinforces the need for Kore.ai's advanced, governed, and analyst-validated agentic platform. We can position ABL's deterministic execution and compile-time validation as key differentiators that build trust and overcome the limitations of basic, unreliable chatbots.

## Watch for
*   Customer adoption and actual TCO data for Agentforce's new pay-per-resolution model.
*   ServiceNow's progress in integrating Google Cloud capabilities into their core platform and its impact on their "assists" consumption model.
*   Cresta's ability to translate niche customer wins like the Mets partnership into broader enterprise CX deployments.

## Recommended actions
*   Arm sales with clear messaging on Kore.ai's predictable TCO and granular cost attribution for agentic AI, directly contrasting with the potential unpredictability of new consumption models.
*   Reinforce Kore.ai's analyst Leader status in *CX-specific* conversational AI (Gartner MQ 2025, Forrester Wave Q2 2026), highlighting our differentiation from big-tech generalists and unvalidated startups.
*   Emphasize ABL's compile-time validation and deterministic execution as crucial for building enterprise trust and addressing governance concerns for autonomous agents, especially in regulated industries.
*   Showcase Kore.ai's true omnichannel capabilities (voice and digital) and cross-agent trace trees for managing complex, integrated enterprise workflows.
