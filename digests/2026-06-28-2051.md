## Watch for
*   ServiceNow's "pricing deadline" (July 1, 2026 for old SKUs) could trigger new customer reactions or further pricing adjustments.
*   Agentforce's push for one-day POCs may indicate a strategy to overcome existing pricing/complexity concerns and accelerate adoption.

## Recommended actions
*   Arm sales teams with clear messaging on Kore.ai's TCO predictability and transparent cost attribution, contrasting it with Agentforce's vague outcome-based pricing.
*   Highlight Kore.ai's compile-time validation and deterministic execution (ABL) as a superior governance model for complex, cross-platform agentic workflows compared to ServiceNow's alliance-driven approach.
*   Educate prospects and analysts on the critical distinction between general AI platform MQs and CX-specific Conversational AI MQs, leveraging Kore.ai's Leader status where IBM is a Challenger.
*   Emphasize Kore.ai's ability to build trusted, effective agents that address customer skepticism (e.g., chatbot avoidance), by showcasing our platform's reliability and seamless human escalation.
