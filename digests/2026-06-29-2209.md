# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Monday, 29 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** ServiceNow is making a significant push into agentic AI with a major SI partnership and a focus on governance, while Salesforce expands its Agentforce product line. Five9 continues to address internal stability, reflecting ongoing challenges in the CCaaS market's shift to AI. Kore.ai must emphasize its superior governance, predictable TCO, and pure-play conversational AI leadership to capitalize on these competitors' inherent weaknesses.

## Three signals that matter

Kore.ai can counter by highlighting its Agent Behavior Language (ABL) for compile-time validation and deterministic execution, offering a more robust and proven governance framework than ServiceNow's newer "bet."

### Agentforce Commerce Goes GA Amidst Adoption Push
**What happened —** (2026-06-29) Salesforce announced the general availability of Agentforce Commerce, expanding its agentic platform offerings, and published guidance on the value of a one-day Agentforce Operations POC, as reported by [CX Today](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMihgFBVV95cUxNaUw5dGVNMXBZYndYdjZ5a3JNMlBJMkJzOXNnd3VmY3MxOUMwbkV1Mkp4RjNXc1RGSk02RFBocXpWLW5pVTFicHVWT3AyUjdIM0s1T1RxWEJNNENEc0RiVkVEcHFaanUzRktpM3VxZ3p4bkd1aVdTa1NlVTRMV0VKUEFra1V6QQ?oc=5) and Salesforce.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** Agentforce's product expansion and push for rapid POCs indicate a drive for adoption, but Kore.ai can leverage Agentforce's documented pricing unpredictability ("Flex Credits") and platform lock-in. We should emphasize Kore.ai's clear cost attribution and independent analyst validation as a Conversational AI Leader, where Agentforce is not.

### Five9 Strengthens Executive Team Amidst Ongoing Instability
**What happened —** (2026-06-29) Five9 announced the appointment of Niranjan Vijayaragavan, Rob Hornish, and Sven Linsmaier to its executive team, a move framed as strengthening leadership, as reported by citybiz/marketscreener.com.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** While presented as a positive, these executive changes follow a period of significant organizational instability for Five9 (layoffs, exec purge, new CEO, activist pressure). Kore.ai can position its stable, pure-play agentic platform and predictable cost-per-outcome model as a reliable alternative to Five9's seat-based model and ongoing internal shifts.

## Watch for

*   ServiceNow's AI governance milestone on July 29 and its implications for enterprise adoption and auditability.
*   Further details on Agentforce Commerce's specific CX automation capabilities and customer traction beyond initial announcements.
*   Any shifts in Five9's pricing model or further organizational changes that could indicate deeper instability.

## Recommended actions

*   Arm Sales with messaging emphasizing Kore.ai's superior governance and deterministic execution via ABL, contrasting it with ServiceNow's "AI governance bet" and newer capabilities.
*   Product Marketing should develop content highlighting Kore.ai's predictable cost attribution and analyst leadership in conversational AI, directly challenging Agentforce's pricing complexity and lack of specific CX analyst validation.
*   Analyst Relations should proactively brief Gartner/Forrester on Kore.ai's platform stability and proven enterprise-grade governance, drawing a clear distinction from Five9's ongoing organizational flux.
*   Highlight Kore.ai's full-lifecycle enterprise platform breadth and true omnichannel capabilities in all outbound messaging to differentiate from niche players like Cresta.
