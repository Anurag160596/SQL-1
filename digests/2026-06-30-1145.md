# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Tuesday, 30 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Salesforce is expanding Agentforce's market reach and vertical solutions, while Five9 is attempting to stabilize its leadership amidst ongoing organizational shifts, underscoring the dynamic competitive landscape as incumbents adapt to agentic AI.

## Three signals that matter

### Agentforce Expands Reach and Vertical Focus
**What happened —** Salesforce has made Agentforce Commerce generally available ahead of peak season (2026-06-29) [CX Today], and simultaneously brought Agentforce to the AWS Marketplace in India (2026-06-30) [APAC Media].
**What it means for Kore.ai —** These moves indicate Salesforce's intent to broaden Agentforce's accessibility and target specific industry use cases.

### Five9 Bolsters Executive Leadership for Strategic Pivot
**What happened —** Five9 has appointed Niranjan Vijayaragavan as CTO, Rob Hornish as Chief Sales Officer, and Sven Linsmaier as Executive VP, Transformation and Strategy (2026-06-29) [CMSWire].
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This significant executive team strengthening suggests Five9 is attempting to stabilize its organization and accelerate its "Agentic CX" pivot. Kore.ai can leverage Five9's ongoing organizational instability by highlighting Kore.ai's stable, AI-native platform and cost-per-outcome advantages.
