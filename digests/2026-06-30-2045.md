# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Tuesday, 30 June 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Genesys's acquisition of Pinkfish signals a continued push for deeper contact center automation capabilities, potentially intensifying competition in specialized AI functions. Salesforce's expansion of Agentforce into India and their acquisition of Fin for AI agent offerings highlight a strategic focus on broadening their AI agent ecosystem and market reach, creating a competitive imperative for Kore.ai to emphasize its platform's comprehensive, unified approach.

## Three signals that matter

**What it means for Kore.ai —** This move by Genesys to acquire specialized automation capabilities underscores a market trend towards deepening AI functionality within CCaaS. Kore.ai's unified, agentic platform approach offers a more integrated and robust solution than bolted-on acquisitions.

**What happened —** Salesforce announced the availability of Agentforce in AWS Marketplace in India on 2026-06-30 [varindia.com](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMiqAFBVV95cUxNMnpPUTVOX2F0Ty1uYkxOeFVZSHJrcmxCU0xWZGFCWTdfS0otamM0ZVFVaWRjVlpla1dxbWhSVUptSGRMdDRWdHd2d2dRbzBHVHlWdnRfM3ViVjd6OXJhbHl4RTJhM2FyS2JvVmNpQVVKX0I0WXlDR2h2SVRNa041cGhYbTJpMUt6dFdqZUJjbnBIeGczU3lSSll3UGdjMmplZFNmSVNNQWY?oc=5).
**What it means for Kore.ai —** Salesforce's continued expansion of Agentforce, particularly into key international markets, highlights their aggressive strategy to embed AI agents across their ecosystem. Kore.ai's strength lies in its platform-agnostic, enterprise-grade conversational AI and agentic capabilities, which offer a more flexible and powerful alternative to platform-specific solutions.

### Salesforce to Acquire Fin for AI Agent Expansion
**What happened —** Salesforce announced its intent to acquire Fin for US$3.6 billion to expand its AI agent offerings on 2026-06-30 [Fintech Singapore](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMibEFVX3lxTE5KTEhlZkhDWmQ4b2MzS3lzenk3S2hEZV9MdV9VaFkxTE1KU2U0eUI5TWlkMUVfem1KZXhKanlRZjNTY0dKRW5vSTlMQnpRdjR2TnJQZUg5WmMzWFJSV2lyLVZBdUNicXROSHV3cg?oc=5).

Kore.ai's established leadership in conversational AI positions it as a superior choice for enterprises seeking a proven, scalable, and unified agentic platform rather than relying on a piecemeal acquisition strategy.
