**What happened —** Genesys announced its acquisition of Pinkfish, a company specializing in AI automation for contact centers, on (2026-07-01) IT Brief Asia. The acquisition aims to speed up Genesys's contact center AI deployments.
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This acquisition reinforces the market trend of CCaaS incumbents bolting on AI capabilities, often through M&A, which can lead to fragmented architectures and integration challenges. Kore.ai's unified, AI-native platform offers a more cohesive and robust solution.
