## Watch for
- Continued integration of acquired companies (e.g., Moveworks into ServiceNow's Otto) and their impact on platform roadmaps.
- Any further analyst evaluations that shift rankings in the Conversational AI for Customer Service space.
- Competitors' efforts to address pricing unpredictability as AI agent usage scales.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **proven, unified omnichannel platform** and its leadership in independent analyst evaluations (Gartner, Forrester) as a counter to fragmented or voice-first offerings.
- Highlight the **deterministic execution and compile-time validation** of Kore.ai's ABL as critical for enterprise governance and reliability, especially against platforms with complex, multi-vendor agent management.
- Arm sales teams with specific questions that probe competitors' **TCO predictability and true omnichannel capabilities** to expose gaps against Kore.ai's integrated stack.
- Reinforce messaging around Kore.ai's **provider-agnostic model tiering** as a strategic advantage over vendor-specific or complex "control plane" approaches.
