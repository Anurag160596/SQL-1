# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Monday, 06 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are aggressively pushing AI agent capabilities, with Salesforce announcing a major Agentforce release and ServiceNow integrating acquisitions. However, gaps in pricing predictability, true omnichannel depth, and independent analyst validation persist, offering clear openings for Kore.ai's proven, enterprise-grade platform.

## Watch for
- Salesforce's continued rollout and adoption of Agentforce, particularly in voice and contact center use cases.
- ServiceNow's ability to integrate its recent acquisitions and address market doubts regarding its AI strategy.
- Any further analyst validation or lack thereof for Salesforce's Agentforce in dedicated CX AI evaluations.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **proven analyst leadership** in Conversational AI for Customer Service (Forrester Wave, Gartner MQ) as a key differentiator against platform players.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **deterministic ABL and clear cost-per-outcome model** to counter the pricing unpredictability and potential for "resolution ambiguity" emerging from competitors like Salesforce.
- Reinforce Kore.ai's **true omnichannel capabilities**, contrasting with competitors that may have voice-first or bolt-on digital channels.
- Arm Sales with specific questions around **governance, auditability, and cost predictability** when engaging with prospects evaluating Salesforce Agentforce.
