# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Wednesday, 08 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are pushing agentic AI capabilities, but often as add-ons to existing platforms or with unclear pricing models. Salesforce's Agentforce continues its aggressive rollout, highlighting a need for clear pricing and omnichannel depth, while Five9's strategic alliance with ServiceNow signals a defensive move against broader platform plays.

## Watch for
*   Continued integration of Agentforce into Salesforce's commerce and Slack offerings, potentially creating deeper platform lock-in.
*   Any further details on the specific capabilities and limitations of the Five9-ServiceNow alliance as it develops.
*   The impact of Accenture's partnership with ServiceNow on broader AI adoption trends in the MENA region.

## Recommended actions
*   Emphasize Kore.ai's analyst leadership in conversational AI for customer service (Gartner, Forrester) to counter platform-centric narratives.
*   Highlight ABL's compile-time validation and deterministic execution as key differentiators against the potential for agentic AI unpredictability and cost overruns.
*   Arm sales teams with questions that probe the true omnichannel capabilities and pricing transparency of integrated platform plays.
*   Reinforce Kore.ai's unified, AI-native platform advantage against the trend of bolted-on AI solutions from CCaaS incumbents.
