## Watch for
- Salesforce's continued efforts to prove Agentforce's CX value proposition amidst analyst skepticism.
- NICE (Cognigy)'s integration progress and roadmap clarity post-acquisition.
- Cresta's adoption of its new "Conductor" developer-native agent build environment.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **analyst-validated leadership** in Conversational AI for Customer Service (Gartner, Forrester) as a key differentiator against competitors lacking this proof.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **deterministic execution and compile-time validation** (ABL) as a critical advantage over competitors whose prompt-based or less-governed approaches risk production failures and auditability issues.
- Arm sales with messaging that contrasts Kore.ai's **predictable, outcome-based TCO** against the consumption-based pricing and seat-model vulnerabilities of competitors like Salesforce and Five9.
- Continue to monitor and articulate the value of Kore.ai's **true omnichannel capabilities** against voice-first or digital-only solutions.
