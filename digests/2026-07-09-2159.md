## Watch for
- Cresta's continued emphasis on agent training and deployment solutions.
- Genesys's evolving narrative around AI adoption and its potential impact on TCO.
- ServiceNow's continued integration of Moveworks and its impact on their agentic AI strategy.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **compile-time validation** and **deterministic execution** in sales conversations, directly addressing the production failures and unpredictability highlighted by Genesys and Cresta.
- Reinforce Kore.ai's **Leader placement in Gartner Conversational AI and Forrester CAI for Customer Service** to counter ServiceNow's ITSM-centric positioning and highlight our purpose-built CX platform.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **predictable cost attribution** (per-agent/intent/tool) versus the AI token overage risks and opaque pricing models of competitors like Genesys.
- Leverage the **"AI Agent Orchestration"** narrative by showcasing Kore.ai's ABL as the robust, governed foundation for complex agentic workflows, contrasting with competitors' more fragmented or prompt-dependent approaches.
