# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Friday, 10 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are doubling down on AI agent capabilities, but gaps in enterprise-grade governance, true omnichannel, and predictable TCO persist. Cresta's new training simulator highlights a focus on agent enablement, while Genesys's partner ecosystem expansion signals a continued effort to bolster its agentic AI offerings. Kore.ai's ABL and compile-time validation remain key differentiators against emerging complexity and potential reliability issues.
