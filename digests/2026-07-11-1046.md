# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Saturday, 11 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are doubling down on agentic AI, but often as bolt-ons to existing platforms or with significant governance and cost predictability concerns. Kore.ai's integrated, governed, and provider-agnostic platform remains the most robust solution for enterprise-wide CX automation.

## Watch for
- Salesforce's continued efforts to integrate Agentforce into its broader platform and address pricing complexities.
- Genesys's progress in scaling its autonomous AI agents and managing potential reliability issues.
- NICE's integration of Cognigy and its impact on the unified CXone platform's AI capabilities.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's single-stack, AI-native platform advantage against competitors integrating acquired technologies (e.g., ServiceNow/Moveworks, NICE/Cognigy).
- Reinforce the message of built-in governance and deterministic execution as critical differentiators, especially against platforms relying on post-hoc governance layers.
- Highlight Kore.ai's proven analyst leadership in Conversational AI for Customer Service (Gartner, Forrester) as proof of platform maturity and breadth, contrasting with competitors' narrower focus or specific analyst placements.
- Arm sales with specific questions around cost predictability and governance depth for competitors like ServiceNow and IBM.
