> **Bottom line:** Competitors are increasingly highlighting their AI agent capabilities, but significant gaps remain in enterprise-grade governance, true omnichannel depth, and predictable TCO. Kore.ai's ABL and proven analyst leadership provide a strong counter to these emerging weaknesses.

## Watch for
- Continued consolidation and integration challenges for acquired entities (e.g., NICE/Cognigy).
- Evolving pricing models from vendors attempting to balance consumption and predictability.
- Further customer churn from platforms perceived as overly complex or lacking specific CX focus.

## Recommended actions
- Arm Sales with messaging that highlights Kore.ai's **proven analyst leadership** in Conversational AI for CX, contrasting with platform vendors' broader but less validated AI plays.
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **ABL advantage** in addressing the "deployment gap" and ensuring deterministic, auditable agent behavior, directly countering competitors' reliance on prompt-based or less validated approaches.
- Position Kore.ai as the **governed, cost-predictable foundation** for enterprise AI expansion, directly addressing CFO concerns about "burning out" AI budgets.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **true omnichannel capabilities** as a key differentiator against voice-first or digital-only solutions.
- Continue to seed the narrative with analysts that **pure-play CX agentic AI platforms** like Kore.ai lead the market in specialized CX AI evaluations.
