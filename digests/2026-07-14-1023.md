# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Tuesday, 14 July 2026)

Sierra's aggressive expansion into Japan highlights its global ambitions, while ServiceNow's strategic partnerships underscore its push to integrate AI deeply into enterprise workflows. Kore.ai should leverage its proven analyst leadership and robust, omnichannel platform to differentiate against these expanding narratives.

## Watch for
- Continued expansion of Sierra's global partnerships and enterprise customer wins.
- ServiceNow's integration of Moveworks and its impact on their agentic AI strategy for CX.
- Any further analyst validation or shifts in the competitive landscape for pure-play CX AI platforms.

## Recommended actions
- Arm Sales with messaging that highlights Kore.ai's proven leadership in *Conversational AI for Customer Service* evaluations, directly contrasting with competitors' broader enterprise AI or ITSM focus.
- Seed analyst relations with data points emphasizing Kore.ai's robust, omnichannel platform and compile-time validation as key differentiators against prompt-based or voice-first solutions.
- Develop targeted collateral showcasing Kore.ai's ability to deliver predictable TCO and ROI for enterprise-scale CX automation, addressing concerns around consumption-based pricing models.
- Continue to emphasize the "one stack" advantage of Kore.ai for true omnichannel CX, differentiating from competitors with bolted-on or voice-first approaches.
- Monitor competitor moves in emerging markets like Japan to inform Kore.ai's international expansion strategy and value proposition.
