> **Bottom line:** Competitors Sierra and NICE are making significant moves to bolster their agentic AI platforms, with Sierra securing a major distribution partnership in Japan and NICE integrating its Cognigy acquisition. ## Watch for
- Continued analyst evaluations of Sierra's platform breadth and enterprise readiness.
- NICE's progress in fully unifying Cognigy's capabilities within the CXone ecosystem.
- Salesforce's ongoing efforts to simplify Agentforce pricing and demonstrate clear ROI beyond initial adoption.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's analyst-validated leadership in Conversational AI for Customer Service (Forrester Wave Q2 2026) as a differentiator against less validated competitors like Sierra.
- Highlight Kore.ai's ABL for its compile-time validation and deterministic execution, contrasting it with the potential integration complexities and roadmap ambiguities of acquired platforms like Cognigy within NICE.
- Reinforce Kore.ai's transparent, outcome-based pricing model as a key advantage over consumption-heavy models like Salesforce's Agentforce, particularly for predictable TCO in enterprise deployments.
- Continue to position Kore.ai as the unified, enterprise-grade platform that avoids the "two AI lineages" challenge presented by acquisitions like NICE/Cognigy.
