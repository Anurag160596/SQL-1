# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Wednesday, 15 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are aggressively pushing AI agent capabilities and partnerships, with Sierra securing a major exclusive partnership in Japan and NICE (Cognigy) embedding its AI into core banking workflows, highlighting the increasing demand for integrated, enterprise-grade agentic solutions. Kore.ai's strength in true omnichannel and analyst-validated leadership in conversational AI for CX remains a key differentiator against these moves.

### NICE (Cognigy) Embeds Agentic AI into Banco do Brasil's Core Banking
**What happened —** (2026-07-14) Banco do Brasil is deploying NICE Agentic AI to transform its relationship banking workflows and enhance customer engagement, embedding AI agents into its core operational processes. FF News
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This move highlights NICE's strategy to integrate Cognigy's capabilities into existing enterprise workflows, particularly in regulated industries like banking. Kore.ai's ABL-driven platform offers compile-time validation and deterministic execution, which are critical for regulated environments and provide a stronger guarantee of compliance and reliability than a bolt-on solution.

## Watch for
- Continued focus from Sierra on expanding its partnership network globally.
- Deeper integration and customer proof points from NICE (Cognigy) in highly regulated sectors.
- ServiceNow's continued efforts to unify its AI offerings under the "Otto" umbrella and demonstrate clear ROI.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **proven, enterprise-grade omnichannel capabilities** and **global deployment readiness** in all sales and marketing engagements, directly contrasting with voice-first or regionally focused competitors.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **analyst-validated leadership** in Conversational AI for Customer Service (Gartner MQ, Forrester Wave) as a key differentiator against competitors with less independent validation or recognition in adjacent categories.
- Reinforce the value of Kore.ai's **Agent Behavior Language (ABL)** for its compile-time validation and deterministic execution, especially when engaging with prospects in regulated industries like banking.
- Monitor competitor pricing models closely, particularly consumption-based or token-based approaches, and proactively position Kore.ai's transparent and predictable TCO.
