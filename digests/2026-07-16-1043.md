# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Thursday, 16 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are increasingly highlighting their AI capabilities, but often as bolt-ons to existing platforms or with unproven enterprise-grade governance. Kore.ai's ABL and analyst validation remain critical differentiators against the hype.

## Watch for
- Continued integration progress and customer proof points for NICE Cognigy within the CXone suite.
- Any further analyst commentary or market reaction to the KeyBanc report on Salesforce Agentforce adoption.
- Expansion of Decagon's reported enterprise customer base and any new voice capabilities.

## Recommended actions
- **Amplify messaging** on Kore.ai's unified, AI-native platform architecture and its proven enterprise-grade governance, directly contrasting with the integration challenges of acquired technologies.
- **Arm Sales** with specific questions about the roadmap clarity and potential for AI lineage overlap when prospects evaluate NICE Cognigy.
- **Reinforce the narrative** of Kore.ai's leadership in independent analyst evaluations for Conversational AI for Customer Service, especially against platforms like Salesforce Agentforce that lack this specific validation.
- **Highlight ABL's compile-time validation** as a critical differentiator for achieving true resolution and deterministic behavior, especially for regulated industries, in response to Parloa's emphasis on resolution.
