## Watch for
- Continued analyst evaluations of agentic AI platforms, particularly for CX-specific use cases.
- Any further clarification on NICE/Cognigy's integration roadmap and how it impacts their agentic AI offering.
- Deeper insights into the adoption challenges for Salesforce Agentforce and their impact on enterprise trust.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **proven leadership in Conversational AI for Customer Service** in analyst and customer conversations, contrasting with competitors' narrower focus or CCaaS-centric evaluations.
- Highlight the **predictability and TCO advantages** of Kore.ai's platform-native, provider-agnostic approach versus the credit-consumption models and potential platform lock-in of competitors.
- Arm Sales with specific questions that probe **integration complexity and roadmap clarity** for competitors like NICE/Cognigy, and **adoption proof points** for Salesforce Agentforce.
- Reinforce the message of **deterministic execution and robust governance** via ABL as critical differentiators for regulated industries, directly countering the risks of probabilistic LLM agents.
