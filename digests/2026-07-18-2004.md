# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Saturday, 18 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors continue to emphasize agentic AI capabilities, but critical gaps in enterprise-grade validation, true omnichannel depth, and predictable TCO persist, creating clear openings for Kore.ai's proven platform. - Any further analyst commentary on the true enterprise adoption and ROI of Salesforce's Agentforce.

## Recommended actions
- Emphasize Kore.ai's **Leader placement in key CX conversational AI evaluations** (Gartner, Forrester) to counter competitors' platform-centric claims.
- Highlight Kore.ai's **proven omnichannel capabilities** (voice and digital) versus competitors with voice-first or internal-facing AI solutions.
- Arm sales with specific talking points on the **predictability and cost-effectiveness of Kore.ai's outcome-based pricing** compared to consumption-based or seat-model pressures.
- Reinforce the message of **enterprise-grade governance and determinism** as critical differentiators for regulated industries, contrasting with the inherent risks of prompt-centric approaches.
