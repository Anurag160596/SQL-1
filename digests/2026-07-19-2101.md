- Continued analyst scrutiny on Salesforce's Agentforce pricing and product maturity.

- Emphasize Kore.ai's analyst leadership in conversational AI for CX (Gartner, Forrester) to counter competitor claims and highlight platform maturity.
- Arm Sales with talking points that contrast Kore.ai's predictable, outcome-based pricing against the "credit anxiety" and TCO unpredictability of consumption-based models like Salesforce Agentforce.
