# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Wednesday, 22 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Five9's significant executive leadership changes signal continued internal flux and potential roadmap uncertainty, presenting an opportunity for Kore.ai to highlight stability. Meanwhile, Salesforce's Agentforce secures a notable customer win with Formula 1, demonstrating traction in CX, while Genesys deepens its strategic AI collaboration with AWS, reinforcing its reliance on external cloud infrastructure for core AI innovation.

## Three signals that matter

**What it means for Kore.ai —** This continued organizational churn at Five9, following multiple layoff rounds and a new CEO, creates an opening to position Kore.ai as a stable, focused, and analyst-validated platform for long-term enterprise AI investments, contrasting with Five9's internal pressures and seat-based model challenges.

### Salesforce Agentforce Secures Marquee Customer Validation
**What happened —** (2026-07-21) Formula 1® reported resolving race-day issues 80% faster using Agentforce Service, showcasing a strong customer success story for Salesforce's agentic platform in a high-stakes customer experience environment. [Salesforce](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMiggFBVV95cUxQWHpZQUQ4cWRLV3c0VzFwMXlHZ2JEQ1NucDEyTHA1Y3BJb0RnVTBrc1l6OExaWk84YWRfeTBsVWp5aUZmNVJDRlFyWFdkTlIxdHRRLUp3Vzg0TmhGMHNyNHBfRGRrTGtMTkVnZmdpckN0dmpFTzBGWUkzTFN1RVY0N21n?oc=5)
**What it means for Kore.ai —** This win validates the demand for agentic AI in CX. Kore.ai must proactively counter Agentforce's perceived momentum by highlighting our superior analyst validation as a Leader in *conversational AI for CX* (where Salesforce is not), and by emphasizing Kore.ai's predictable TCO and model-agnostic flexibility against Agentforce's pricing unpredictability and platform lock-in.

### Genesys Deepens AWS Partnership for AI Innovation
**What happened —** (2026-07-22) Genesys announced a deepened strategic collaboration with AWS to accelerate global AI innovation, indicating a continued reliance on hyperscaler infrastructure for their core AI development and deployment. Business Wire
**What it means for Kore.ai —** While showing commitment to AI, this partnership underscores Genesys's "bolted-on" AI approach and dependence on external cloud providers for fundamental AI capabilities, which has previously led to reliability issues. Kore.ai can leverage its ABL's deterministic execution, compile-time validation, and model-agnostic tiering to offer superior governance, auditability, and resilience compared to Genesys's AI, which is integrated into a CCaaS platform.
