# Competitive Signal Report — Enterprise AI Platform (Thursday, 23 July 2026)

> **Bottom line:** Competitors are aggressively pushing AI agent capabilities, with Salesforce and ServiceNow highlighting new B2B commerce and employee service applications, respectively. While Genesys deepens its AWS collaboration, the core threat remains the continued analyst validation gap for many players against Kore.ai's leadership in the conversational AI for customer service market.
